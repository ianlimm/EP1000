Music Box

[Click here to see how i CAD it]

To start off laser cutting my music box, i transfered all the parts into a dxf file using the programme "Libre CAD". For the rose picture on the side which will be engraved, i drew the design using the programme "InkScape".

I then transfered both files into another programme on the laser cutter, to prepare the machine to cut out my pieces.

The pieces were then cut and engraved.

After cutting the pieces out, i drilled a hole on the right side of the music box, which the handle will poke out.

The music box is then assembled together with UHU glue, finalizing it.

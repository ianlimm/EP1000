What is EP1000?

EP1000 is an elective module that will teach us how to use Digital Fabrication techniques in designing and creating prototypes. In the process we will learn how to document and publish our work. We will use Computer Aided Design techniques, 3D printing and laser cutting methods to develop our prototypes. In addition, we will be able use a microcontroller to add intelligence to our project.
